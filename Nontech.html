Oru Kadha sollata sir? Rules and Regulations

    Maximum 1 member
  • You must create a story with some random pictures which will be presented to you during the time of the competition.
  • Any form of obscenity in speech will lead to direct disqualification.
  • Humor, spontaneity and creativity will be appreciated.

    • Word War III Rules and Regulations

    • You will be split into different teams.
    • Language:English
    • The topic will be given only on the day of competition.
    • The topics may pertain to general aspects of technology.
    • Incase of any misconduct or foul language, it may result in the disqualification of the participant.
    • Participants will be informed if they belong to the proposition/opposition team based on the topic and the pool they belong to, only on the day of competition.
    • 10 Minutes time will be given to both the proposition and opposition team along with separate meet links to brainstorm points with their respective team mates.
    • The first round of debating will go for 15 minutes.
    • Each battle on the given topic has two or three constructive speeches, and two to three rebuttal speeches.
    • The affirmative tries to prove a point or put forth a point to the opposition team, while the opposition team tries to disprove it.
    • He who asserts must prove. In order to establish an assertion, the team must support it with enough evidence and logic to convince an intelligent but previously uninformed person that it is more reasonable to believe the assertion than to disbelieve it.
    • Every point put forth by the proposition /opposition team and debated may last for only 3 minutes.
    • The participants mustn't cross the given allotted time.
    • Round 2 will happen in the same manner and the best debaters from each pool will be selected for the finals.
    • Judges may have questions/answer sessions if they choose.
    • Judges decision will be final.
